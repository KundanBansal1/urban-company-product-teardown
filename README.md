# Urban Company – Product Teardown & Growth Strategy

A product teardown and growth strategy case study focused on solving one of Urban Company's key business challenges:

> **Increase cross-category bookings to maximize Customer Lifetime Value (LTV).**

This repository contains a complete product analysis covering customer research, behavioral insights, UX evaluation, competitor benchmarking, and an AI-powered product redesign.

---

# Problem Statement

Urban Company has successfully established trust across multiple home service categories.

However,

- Most customers associate Urban Company with only one category.
- Users rarely explore services beyond their first booking.
- Cross-category adoption remains significantly low.

The challenge is not trust.

It is **discovery and habit formation.**

---

# Objectives

- Increase cross-category bookings
- Improve customer retention
- Increase Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
- Create proactive service discovery
- Build recurring engagement instead of transactional usage

---

# Research Process

The teardown combines multiple research approaches:

- User Interviews
- Survey Responses
- Reddit discussions
- Existing customer behavior analysis
- Booking flow evaluation
- Competitor benchmarking
- Product heuristics

---

# Key Insights

### User Behavior

- Users mentally categorize Urban Company as a salon app or cleaning app rather than a complete home services platform.
- Discovery largely stops after the first successful booking.
- Cross-category recommendations are weak throughout the booking flow.

---

### Opportunity Gap

The analysis identifies three major problems:

- No proactive discovery
- No habit formation
- Limited cross-category awareness

---

# Solution: HomeCare OS

The proposed solution transforms Urban Company from a transactional booking platform into a proactive home management assistant.

Core components include:

### AI Discovery Assistant

Natural language interface that recommends relevant household services based on user context.

### Occasion-Based Bundles

Smart bundled recommendations combining complementary services for seasonal and recurring needs.

### Unified Home Dashboard

A centralized dashboard showing:

- Home Health Score
- Upcoming maintenance
- Appliance history
- Personalized reminders
- Service recommendations
